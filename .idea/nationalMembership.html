National Membership

We want you to become a member of the most dynamic group of students, professionals, advisors, and friends who are working toward eliminating disparities in education and achievement in engineering. Becoming a national member is the easiest way to join our efforts in fulfilling the mission. Regardless of where you might fit on the engineering life cycle, NSBE has a place for you.

Membership Types

  • NSBE Jr: Pre-college (Grades 6-12): 5 per year!
  • Collegiate: Undergraduate and Graduate students 15 per year!
  • Preofessional: technical professional and graduate students 50 per year!
  • Lifetime: Make the ULTIMATE comitment to NSBE. Dare to SOAR in RARE AIR...
  • International: For individuals who reside outside of the U.S. and Canada. International membership rates apply for all catergories except Lifetime Membership

Member benefits

  • Access to an unparalleled network of diverse engineers and scientists
  • Access to over $500,00 worth of scholarships
  • Online Job Placement Center
  • Access to 7 Annual Career Fairs through Fall Regional Conferences and NSBE’s Annual Convention
  • Annual subscription to NSBE’s magazines
  • Discounted registration at NSBE Conventions
  • Product discounts
